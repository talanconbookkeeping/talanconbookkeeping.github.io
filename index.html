About Us

Hello and welcome to our page! As previous business owners of an establishment, we understand why you may be here.

Services

Monthly Bookkeeping

We do ongoing recording, categorizing, and reconciling of your business's financial transactions on a monthly basis. It ensures your financial records are consistently up to date, providing a clear picture of your company's health and eliminating last-minute stress during tax season.

Profit and Loss

We provide the financial report that summarizes your company's revenues, costs, and expenses over a specific period (e.g., monthly, quarterly, or yearly). Its primary purpose is to calculate your net profit or loss.

Balance Sheet

This report details what the business owns (assets), what it owes (liabilities), and the owner's or shareholders' stake in the business (equity).

Bank Reconciliation

We compare your internal financial records with your monthly bank statement. This will help identify discrepancies, correct errors, and ensure both balances match.

Accounts Payable

We manage your company's short-term debts and outstanding bills owed to vendors, suppliers, and service providers. From invoice processing to verification and approval to payment execution and finally ledger management.

Accounts Receivable

We track outstanding invoices from generation to final collection, ensuring a healthy cash flow. From invoice generation to payment processing to collections and finally reconciliation.

QuickBooks Setup

We help you set up your QuickBooks account. Plus, training of QuickBook to help you better understand the software.

Clean up

We will gather all financial documents, reconcile every bank and credit card statement, and recategorize past transactions to ensure accuracy.

Catch up

We will update and reconcile past-due financial records to bring your books back on track.
